Scam warning signs
⚡
Urgency & pressure
"Act now or your account will be closed!" is designed to rush you into a decision before you think clearly.
🔗
Fake domains
Scammers use domains like amaz0n.com or paypa1-support.net. Always look closely at the full URL before clicking.
🎁
Unexpected prizes
If you didn't enter a competition, you didn't win one. Free gift offers are almost always bait.
🔐
Requests for info
No legitimate bank or company will ever ask for your password or full card number over email or text.
👤
Impersonation
Scammers pretend to be banks, Amazon, or even family. Check the actual sender email, not just the display name.
📎
Unexpected attachments
Never open attachments from unknown senders. Even PDFs can contain malware that steals your data silently.
